A selection of my recent design work.

Flora Bora

Flora Bora is a scented candle company based in Auckland, New Zealand. The design for the logo is quirky and the floral designs stand out againts a minimilistic white background. The scents are fresh and bring the senses of nature and the outdoors into the home.

Oracle

Oracle is an Irish Handcrafted jewellery business based in Scarriff, Co. Clare. The designs are inspired by flora from the West of Ireland and the packaging designs is reflective of this theme.Ivy greens, purple tones from heather and yellow reminiscent of dandelions bring the personality of the business to the branding.

Premier

Premier is a car valeting business based in Limerick. The client wanted a bold black background and a logo that described their services as gold standard. This logo is shining, bold and makes a statement much like the cars that are serviced at Premier.

Melterioristic

Melterioristic is an Irish interior design business. The theme for the brand had to be current and also give a sense of art deco. I did this by keeping the lines simple and incorporating the design of a peacock feather. This logo is similar to design pieces featured in their portfolio and gives a strong sense of their brand.

Haggy Hats

Haggy Hats is a small business specialising in woolen hats and accessories in the Beara Peninsula. Their products are colorful and quirky just like this logo design.

La Boheme Coffee

La Boheme Coffee wanted a fun recognisable logo that would give a sense of its bohemian roots and stand out from the crowd, Orange, brown and neutral tones are nostalgic and the trypography is rustic which suited the companys aesthetic ideas.
